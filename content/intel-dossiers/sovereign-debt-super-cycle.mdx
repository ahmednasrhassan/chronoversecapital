# Executive Summary

The structural fragility of sovereign debt markets has reached an inflection point. As central bank balance sheets undergo quantitative tightening (QT), collateral scarcity in Tier-1 funding markets is creating asymmetric tail risks across global asset classes.

## Systemic Risk Dynamics

1. **Collateral Scarcity:** Primary dealer balance sheets are increasingly constraint-bound.
2. **Liquidity Fragmentation:** Cross-border capital flows are shifting toward non-sovereign hard assets.
3. **Monetary Pivot Vectors:** Debt servicing costs necessitate a medium-term expansion of central bank balance sheets.

> "The expansion of sovereign debt cycles historically precedes fundamental shifts in reserve asset preferences."

### Strategic Outlook & Positioning

We maintain a high-conviction outlook favoring hard monetary assets and short-duration duration hedges as systemic liquidity bottlenecks manifest across major clearing channels.
